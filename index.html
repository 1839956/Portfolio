A bit about who I am

I'm one of those people who gets equally excited about a well-structured product roadmap and a well-trained ML model. My background is a blend of Computer Science (AI) and an MBA from ESADE — which basically means I can talk to engineers and executives in the same meeting without anyone feeling lost.

Over the past four years, I've worked across consulting, pharma, and healthtech.
